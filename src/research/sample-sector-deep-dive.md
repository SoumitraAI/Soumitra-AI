## Executive summary

This is where your real report will open: three to five tight paragraphs a busy reader could stop after and still take away your core argument. State the question the report answers, your headline findings, and what you'd advise.

## 1. Context

Set out the sector or organisation as it stands: market structure, the pressures it faces, and why AI is now a board-level question rather than an IT one.

## 2. Findings

The body of the analysis. Use numbered sections so the report can be referenced in discussion ("see section 2.3"). Evidence, examples and reasoning live here.

## 3. Implications

What the findings mean for the people who run things: where value is likely to move, what becomes possible, what becomes risky, and over what time horizon.

## 4. Recommendations

What you would actually do — sequenced, realistic, and honest about uncertainty.
