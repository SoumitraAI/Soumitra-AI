This site exists for one reason: most writing about AI is either breathless or dismissive, and very little of it helps anyone actually *decide* anything.

I want Soumitra.AI to sit in the gap. Two kinds of work will live here:

**The blog** — shorter pieces on developments in AI as they happen. Not news summaries; you can get those anywhere. The aim is interpretation: what a development means, who it affects, and what I'd be doing about it if I were in their seat.

**The research library** — longer, consulting-grade reports. Sector deep dives, organisational strategy reviews, opportunity assessments. The kind of document that would normally sit behind an engagement fee, published in the open.

If that sounds useful, the [research library](/research/) is the best place to start.

*— Soumitra*
